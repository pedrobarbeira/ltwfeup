A New Semester at FEUP

A new semester just started in the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto! This semester will bring many challenges and, hopefully, many chances for success as well.

Our Engineers are set and ready to go. There's a mew archdemon in campus - one that's particularly noisy and hard to deal with. Many students have been trying to tear down this old fiend since the start of the school year, but had no avail in it.

Fortunately, the new courses this semester focus on just that. With a new developed technology, developed by FEUP's graduate students, our hopes rose up again. We expect to trap this archfiend into a vessel, in order to contain it's negative impact on it's surroundings. Through the use of top quality materials and high-end tech, the chaotic energies of this demon will be put to good use.

The basis of the tech is similar to what we can find in nuclear powerplants all over the world. By poking it with a stick, we expect to incur it's anger, making it growl and try to attack us with it's fiery breath. Once the flames start pouring out, they'll be directed through a state of the art adamantium pipe, heating up water, which in turn will boil. The vapors will rotate a turbine, thus generating energy.

We expect that the transmutation of this archfiend will provide enough energy to power the whole campus of the Univeristy of Porto for a whole semester! And we have to thank our amazing students here at FEUP for making this dream a reality.
